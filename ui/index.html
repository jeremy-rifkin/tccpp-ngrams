Together C & C++ Ngrams Viewer

This is a tool for looking at frequencies of words and phrases over time on the Together C & C++ discord server. The data is anonymized and reasonably sanitized. Most punctuation is eliminated as part of tokenization. You can do queries such as internal compiler error, SFINAE, RAII, C is *, C++*, etc. Standard glob syntax is supported.
